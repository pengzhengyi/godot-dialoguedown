DialogueDown — Compilation Visualization

The Source tab shows the document with a live preview; each compiler stage appears as an interactive graph in its own tab.

Node details

Click any node in the graph to see the source it was produced from, and a rendered preview.

How to use

Source tab shows the whole document beside a live preview; preview links jump to their headings, and you can drag the divider to re-proportion the two panes.

Click a node (its label or details) to inspect the source it was produced from and a rendered preview.

Click a node's circle to collapse or expand its children.

Drag to pan, scroll to zoom, and drag the divider to resize this panel.

Zoom controls (bottom-right of the graph): + / − to zoom, and click the percentage to reset the view.

Hover a legend entry (top-right) to highlight its nodes; click it to dim or show that type. The count shows how many are present.

Arrow keys move the selection: → first child, ← parent, ↑/↓ siblings; Enter or Space collapses or expands.
